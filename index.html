JESSICA & ERIC

Once upon a time, in the beautiful state of Oregon, two people met for a beer and a Blazer’s game and fell madly, madly in love. The Blazers won by 9 points that night, and Eric and Jessica won by infinity points in their hearts! It was a pretty wild ride after that. The world was about to change in some significant ways, and their love would get them through it all. In the midst of the 2020 Oregon fires, in a pretend Double Dragon (their favorite spot for drinks and scrabble), Jessica asked Eric to be her husband and he said yes! They grew quite impatient waiting for a pesky Pandemic to end, so they decided to do the thing ASAP and celebrate with all of their friends & family later.

THESE CUTIES!

They plan to enjoy a life full of love and food and music and smooching and dancing and disco and friends and family as long as they both shall live.

A NEW FAMILY

This ceremony forms a new family! We’d like to introduce the new Paxtons! Jessica will become a stepmom for the first time. Jude, Eli, Cole, and Seth will be stepsons for the first time. Eric will keep being a badass dad. And we’ll all be welcoming another new family member soon. Friends meet “Flower” (her temporary name), our new lil’ fluffy girl who will be joining the family in April.

A MESSAGE FROM ERIC & JESS

Dearest Friends & Family,

What we wouldn’t give to be celebrating this moment with you all! Every single day, we talk about how grateful we are to have incredible friends and family supporting us with all the love, talks, prayers, encouragement, excitement, joy, and laughter we could ever hope for.

To our families - there is no doubt that we love and support each other so completely because you taught us how. We say it all the time - we come from really good people. We were both raised to value love and kindness over everything. We were carried through life by people with giant hearts, and great humor. These things have been our rock through the tough stuff, and we will constantly lean into them as we move through this life together.

A special shout-out to our wedding party. We didn’t know we’d have to limit our guest list to six, but we couldn’t imagine a better pod. We’ll be missing many, but these 6 people mean sooooo much to us - Brett, Melissa, Zach, Beatrice, Katie, and Sam are the greatest friends who’ve celebrated us every step of the way. Extra extraordinary shouts to Julia for making our cake, and to Osandi for the fragrance curation.

Even though we can’t all be together for this special day, please know that you are all here with us in spirit and we are ALWAYS feeling the love. Thank you for showing up in this new way, and for celebrating with us. We’ll party together soon!

Xoxo
